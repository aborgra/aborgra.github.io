About Me

Prior to beginning my journey in software development, I was working as an Echo/vascular technologist in the healthcare field.

I enjoyed the opportunity that healthcare gave me to help others and make a difference in peoples' lives. It allowed me to solve complex problems using logic while working in a team environment.

When a family member introduced me to software development, I realized that the tech field would allow me to continue to use those skills I enjoyed, while not being so physically wearing on my body.

This started my love of software that has continued to grow with every new technology I encounter.
